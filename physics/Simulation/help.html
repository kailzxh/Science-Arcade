Welcome to marble physics simulation

Explore the exciting world of physics simulations with marble physics simulation! This project is made with the venilla js. Please use a laptop or computer to enjoy all the feautures of this website. However don't worry mobile users as there are still some basic features available for you. Here's a quick guide to help you make the most of your experience:

Gravity

  • When gravity is enabled, marbles will fall towards the bottom of the screen.
  • The marbles will accelerate as they fall, following the laws of gravity.

  • Marbles will collide with obstacles and other marbles.

Friction and Bounce

  • Marbles will experience friction when moving along surfaces, slowing down over time.
  • The elasticity of the marbles determines how much they bounce when they hit surfaces or obstacles.

Experiment with Gravity

Turn gravity on and off to see how it affects the movement of marbles and to create interesting dynamic challenges.

Obstacle Shapes

Different shapes of obstacles will affect the marbles in various ways. Use them creatively to manipulate marble trajectories.
